International Invites

A few weeks ago we posted about our love of Auto Chess and how we were working on our own
standalone version of it. In that same post we promised we would share information about the
game soon. As it turns out, “soon” is today.

Dota Underlords is a new stand-alone game that pits you against seven opponents in a battle of wits
that will have you building, combining, and leveling-up a crew in a battle of dominance for the city
White Spire. In this game, victory is determined not by twitch reflexes, but by superior tactics.
